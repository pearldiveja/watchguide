How to Identify Valuable Watches

Look for these key indicators of valuable watches:

  • Brand: Luxury brands like Rolex, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet retain high value.
  • Condition: Watches in excellent physical and functional condition are worth more.
  • Rarity: Limited edition or discontinued models tend to have higher values.
  • Original Packaging: Presence of original box and paperwork increases value.
  • Market Trends: Check current demand for specific models.
